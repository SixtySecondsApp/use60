What they see

Personal command center

"Welcome, Sarah." A full mock 60 dashboard pre-loaded with their company as a deal, simulated meetings, an AI-written follow-up email about their product, and a deal health score. They're already inside 60 before they sign up.

URL: use60.com/t/{6-char-code} — 21 chars total
Method: Random base62 code → DB lookup (no encryption)
Capacity: 56.8B unique codes (base62 × 6 chars)
Fallback: Invalid code → redirect to homepage
WHY LOOKUP BEATS ENCRYPTION
Shorter URLs (21 vs 35 chars) · Faster (DB lookup vs decrypt) · More secure (nothing to reverse-engineer) · Code IS the tracking ID
